# Twitter DM Bot

Automate the sending of direct messages to people on Twitter's search page with just a few simple commands. This bot leverages Puppeteer to control a Chromium browser, enabling you to reach potential connections without paying for Twitter's API access.

<Note>
  This bot uses your existing Twitter session cookies to authenticate - no API keys or Twitter Developer account required.
</Note>

## What Does It Do?

The Twitter DM Bot crawls Twitter search results (Top, Latest, People, etc.) based on your search query and automatically sends direct messages to profiles that allow DMs from non-followers. It's perfect for:

- Networking and outreach campaigns
- Event promotion to local communities
- Building connections around specific topics or hashtags
- Recruiting or lead generation

## How It Works

1. **Extract Cookies**: Export your Twitter session cookies from your browser
2. **Configure Target**: Set your search query (hashtag, location, or keyword)
3. **Customize Message**: Write the message you want to send
4. **Run the Bot**: Execute `npm start` and watch it work

The bot navigates to Twitter's search page, scrolls through results, and opens each profile to check if messaging is available. It maintains a record of visited profiles to avoid sending duplicate messages.

<Warning>
  Due to Twitter's recent updates, you may need a Twitter Plus subscription to send messages to users who have disabled DMs from unknown people.
</Warning>

## Limitations

- Can only send messages to people who have allowed messages outside of their followers/following
- Requires Twitter Plus subscription for users with restricted DM settings
- Subject to Twitter's rate limits for continuous messaging
